Welcome to the Neoscona developer portal. Learn how to switch on AI workers and build custom automations.

Master Branding

Neoscona is built on a unified platform architecture. Every product shares the same design tokens, authentication layer, and anti-hallucination core.

To integrate Reva specifically, refer to the ../reva directory for engine implementation details, or use our pre-built Neoscona Reva components.

The Console API

You can interact with your AI workers programmatically via the console API. All requests require your ADMIN_TOKEN.

POST /api/upload
Content-Type: multipart/form-data

{
  "file": "business_inventory.pdf",
  "worker": "reva"
}

Anti-Hallucination Core

Ground your workers by uploading your business truth to the Knowledge Base. Our system ensures the AI cross-checks every fact against your specific data before replying.
